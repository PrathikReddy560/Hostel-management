Hostel Rules & Regulations

General Rules

Entry/Exit Timing

Hostel gates close at 10:30 PM on weekdays and 11:30 PM on weekends. Late entry requires prior permission from the warden.

Visitor Policy

Visitors allowed between 9 AM and 8 PM. All visitors must register at the reception. Overnight guests are not permitted.

Mess Timing

Breakfast: 7-9 AM, Lunch: 12-2 PM, Dinner: 7-9 PM. No food will be served outside these hours except in case of illness.

Quiet Hours

Quiet hours are from 10 PM to 6 AM. Please be considerate of others who may be studying or sleeping.

Prohibited Items & Activities

Strictly Prohibited

Alcohol, smoking, drugs, and firearms are strictly prohibited on hostel premises. Violation will result in immediate expulsion.

Electrical Appliances

High-power electrical appliances like heaters, irons, and cooking equipment are not allowed in rooms. Use designated areas.

Property Damage

Students will be charged for any damage to hostel property. Defacing walls, furniture, or fixtures is strictly prohibited.

Disciplinary Action

Repeated rule violations will lead to disciplinary action, including warnings, fines, and potential expulsion from the hostel.

Important Notice

New COVID-19 protocols are in effect. Face masks are recommended in common areas during high-traffic periods. Sanitization stations are available throughout the building.
