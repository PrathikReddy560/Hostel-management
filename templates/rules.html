HOSTEL HUB

Hostel Hub is a state-of-the-art hostel management system designed to enhance student living. Our platform integrates advanced technology with user-centric design to provide a seamless, secure, and community-driven experience for students and wardens.

Our system offers robust features:
  • Student Portal: Manage room assignments, submit maintenance or complaint requests, view meal schedules, and communicate with wardens.
  • Warden Dashboard: Oversee attendance, allocate rooms, track issues, and generate detailed reports.
  • Real-Time Notifications: Stay updated with instant alerts for announcements, events, or schedule changes.
  • Secure Authentication: Role-based access with encrypted credentials ensures data privacy.
  • Payment Integration: Pay hostel fees or fines securely through the portal.
